1. Introduction

SwipSwap (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy and is committed to keeping our community safe. This policy explains how we handle your personal data and how we moderate user-generated content to protect users from abusive or harmful behavior. By using SwipSwap, you agree to this Privacy & Safety Policy and our Terms of Service.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information (e.g., name, email, login credentials).
  • User-Generated Content (e.g., photos, item descriptions, messages you send).
  • Usage Data (e.g., device, app interactions, diagnostics, and analytics).
  • Optional Information you choose to provide (e.g., profile and location details).

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide, personalize, and improve the app and its features.
  • Facilitate listings, trades, connections, and messaging between users.
  • Moderate content and detect, prevent, and respond to policy violations.
  • Communicate with you about updates, security, and support.

4. User-Generated Content Rules

  • You may not upload, share, or post content that is illegal, harmful, abusive, harassing, defamatory, obscene, sexually explicit, or otherwise objectionable.
  • You may not impersonate others, engage in fraud, or violate intellectual property or privacy rights.
  • You are responsible for the content you create and share. You must be at least 17 to use the app.

5. Content Moderation & Reporting

We take active steps to moderate user-generated content, including:

  • Automated filters to detect and block objectionable material.
  • Human review of content that is flagged by users or detected by our systems.
  • Tools for users to report abusive behavior or inappropriate content directly in the app.

How to report: Use the in-app Report option available on listings, profiles, or messages, or contact us at ricky.swipswap@gmail.com. We review reports promptly and take appropriate action.

Block & mute: Users can block or mute other users via the profile or conversation menu to prevent further contact.

6. Consequences of Violations

  • Removal of content that violates this policy or our Terms of Service.
  • Feature restrictions, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans for repeat or severe violations.
  • Reporting of illegal activity to law enforcement where required.

7. Data Sharing & Disclosure

  • With service providers that help us operate the app (e.g., hosting, analytics) under confidentiality and data protection obligations.
  • As required by law, or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, subject to continued protections.

We do not share your personal data for third‑party advertising without your consent.

8. Data Security & Retention

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer period is required by law.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. You may also withdraw consent where applicable. To exercise your rights, contact us at ricky.swipswap@gmail.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy & Safety Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted in the app and/or on our website with the revised date shown above.

  • Email: ricky.swipswap@gmail.com
